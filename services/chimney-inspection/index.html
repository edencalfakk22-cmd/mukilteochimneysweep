Chimney Inspection — your questions

What's the difference between a Level 1 and Level 2 inspection?

Level 1 covers readily accessible areas during a routine sweep. Level 2 adds a video scan of the full flue and is recommended after a chimney fire, a home sale, or any change to your heating appliance.

Do I need an inspection if I rarely use my fireplace?

Yes — Mukilteo's salt air and moisture damage chimneys even when they sit unused, and animals love to nest in idle flues. A periodic check keeps you safe.

Can you inspect before I sell my Mukilteo home?

Absolutely. A documented Level 2 inspection gives buyers confidence and prevents last-minute surprises during the sale.
