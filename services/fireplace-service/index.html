Fireplace & Wood Stove Service — your questions

My wood stove glass keeps blackening — can you help?

Yes. Blackened glass usually points to damp wood, poor draft or worn door gaskets. We diagnose the cause and tune the stove so it burns clean.

Do you service gas fireplaces too?

We do — cleaning, glass and logset service, and pilot and connection checks. Gas units still need annual attention to run safely.

Why does my Mukilteo fireplace smoke into the room?

Often it's a cold flue, a blocked cap, or a draft issue worsened by our damp marine air. We find the real cause and fix the draw.
