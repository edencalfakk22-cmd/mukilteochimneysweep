Creosote Removal & Fire Safety — your questions

How do I know if I have dangerous creosote buildup?

A strong smoky odor, reduced draft, or a shiny black coating in the flue are all warning signs. A camera inspection confirms the stage and amount.

Can damp Mukilteo firewood cause more creosote?

Definitely. Wet wood burns cooler and produces far more creosote. Burning seasoned, dry wood plus annual cleaning keeps buildup in check.

What should I do if I think I had a chimney fire?

Stop using the fireplace and book a Level 2 inspection right away. Chimney fires can crack liners and masonry invisibly, and we'll assess the damage before you relight.
