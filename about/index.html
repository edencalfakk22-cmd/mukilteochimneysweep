About Lighthouse Chimney Co.

Lighthouse Chimney Co. was built around a simple Mukilteo reality: the same salt air and marine weather that make this a beautiful place to live are quietly destroying chimneys all over town. We started serving the waterfront, Old Town and bluff-top neighborhoods because local homeowners deserve a chimney company that actually understands what Possession Sound weather does to brick, crowns and steel caps.

We're a scheduled, appointment-based service — not a 24/7 emergency outfit — which means we show up on time, do careful work, and leave your home cleaner than we found it. Every job comes with an honest written quote and a straight answer about what your chimney really needs before winter arrives.

Why Mukilteo homeowners trust us

  • Marine-climate specialists: stainless caps and waterproofing built for salt air
  • Drop-cloth clean process with HEPA vacuums on every sweep
  • Written, photo-documented inspections — no scare tactics
  • Locally focused on Mukilteo's waterfront, Old Town and bluff homes

Licensed & insured

We are licensed in Washington State and fully insured, and we work to CSIA-style chimney standards — serving homeowners across Mukilteo and the greater Puget Sound.
